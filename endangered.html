Snakes on the edge.

Habitat loss, road mortality, the illegal pet trade, and persecution have pushed hundreds of snake species toward extinction. These eight stories illustrate the wider crisis — tap a card for the full picture of what's being done.

Glossary

  • Endemic: Found nowhere else on Earth.
  • IUCN status: Global conservation risk ranking.
  • Reintroduction: Releasing captive-bred animals back to the wild.
  • Habitat specialist: Needs a very particular environment to survive.
