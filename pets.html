Keeping snakes at home

Good starter snakes.

Not every snake belongs in a terrarium, but these ten species have earned their reputation as calm, manageable, and genuinely rewarding pets. Tap a card for housing notes and a full feeding plan — exactly what to feed, how often, and how much.

Before you buy

Basic care checklist

NeedWhy it matters
Secure, escape-proof enclosureSnakes are expert escape artists and can squeeze through surprisingly small gaps.
Thermal gradient (warm & cool side)As ectotherms, snakes regulate body temperature by moving between zones.
Fresh water & correct humidityNeeded for hydration and healthy shedding (ecdysis).
Appropriately sized preyPrevents regurgitation and choking hazards — see each species' feeding plan above.
A long-term commitmentMany pet snakes live 15–30 years — this is a decades-long responsibility.

Glossary

  • Morph: A selectively bred colour/pattern variant.
  • Ecdysis: The shedding of skin.
  • Ectotherm: Relies on external heat to regulate body temperature.
  • Thermal gradient: A warm-to-cool temperature range in an enclosure.
